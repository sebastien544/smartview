What we do

Your home, looked after

A complete home concierge for absent owners across Hua Hin — we watch over your property, handle the practical stuff, and keep it spotless when you need it.

House watching

Regular visits while you're away — photo report, general check and early problem flagging, so nothing is left to chance.

Property inspection

A single visit with photos plus water, electricity and general condition checks — before you arrive or after a storm.

Key-holding & concierge

Secure key-holding plus help on the ground — meeting contractors, deliveries and the little errands you can't do from abroad.

Airbnb & rental support

Reliable check-in / check-out handling for your short-stay guests, with turnover cleaning and photos on request.

Cleaning on demand Add-on

Condo & villa cleaning whenever you want it — one-off, before arrival, or on a regular schedule. Eco-friendly products as standard.
